Why Choose Sunshine Academy?

We provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for your child's early development and education journey.

Nurturing Environment

Our caring staff creates a warm, loving atmosphere where children feel safe to explore, learn, and grow emotionally and socially.

Play-Based Learning

We combine structured learning with play to develop cognitive, social, emotional, and motor skills through engaging activities.

Experienced Educators

Our qualified teachers have specialized training in early childhood development and primary education with 5+ years of experience.

Healthy Nutrition

Daily nutritious meals prepared on-site with fresh, organic ingredients to support children's growth and development.

Creative Expression

Art, music, drama, and movement activities to encourage imagination, self-expression, and confidence building.

Outdoor Learning

Beautiful playgrounds, gardens, and nature exploration for physical development and environmental awareness.

Admissions Process

Join our Sunshine Academy family through our simple admissions process

Enroll Your Child Today

We're excited that you're considering Sunshine Academy for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and informative, ensuring we find the perfect program for your child's needs.

We accept applications year-round based on availability. Spaces fill quickly, so we encourage you to begin the process as early as possible.

1

School Tour & Meeting

Schedule a personalized tour to see our facilities, meet our staff, and learn about our programs.

2

Submit Application

Complete our online application form and submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school records).

3

Child Assessment

We conduct a friendly assessment to understand your child's developmental level and learning needs.

4

Admission Decision

Receive notification of acceptance and complete enrollment paperwork to secure your child's spot.
